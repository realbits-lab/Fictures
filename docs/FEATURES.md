# Features

**Complete feature guide for Fictures**

## üìù Story Writing & Creation

### Hierarchical Story Structure
Organize your stories with a clear hierarchy:
- **Stories** - Your top-level container
- **Parts** - Optional story sections (Book 1, Act 1, etc.)
- **Chapters** - Story chapters
- **Scenes** - The smallest content unit (where the magic happens)

This structure helps you think about pacing, organization, and gives readers natural break points.

### AI-Powered Writing Assistant

Our AI isn't just autocomplete on steroids. It actually helps you write better:

- **Story Generation**: Uses the Adversity-Triumph Engine methodology to create compelling narrative arcs
- **Scene Content Generation**: Generate dialogue, description, and action that fits your story's tone
- **Character Development**: Get suggestions for character arcs, motivations, and growth
- **Story Analysis**: Receive improvement recommendations based on narrative structure
- **Dialogue Enhancement**: Format and improve conversations to sound natural
- **AI-Generated Images**: Create scene illustrations and character portraits

### Rich Text Editor
- Built on ProseMirror/TipTap for professional-grade editing
- Advanced formatting options (bold, italic, headings, lists, etc.)
- Distraction-free writing mode
- Auto-save every few seconds (because crashes happen)
- Keyboard shortcuts for power users

### Character & Setting Management
- Visual character grid with AI-generated portraits
- Track character details, relationships, and development
- Organize settings and locations
- Cross-reference characters across scenes

### Version Control
- Auto-save functionality (never lose work)
- Track changes over time
- View word count history
- Scene evaluation scores to track improvement

### Story Export
Download your complete story in various formats for backup or publishing elsewhere.

## üìö Reading & Discovery

### Genre-Based Discovery
Browse stories across 7 major genres:
- **Fantasy** - Magic, dragons, epic quests
- **Science Fiction** - Space operas, dystopias, tech noir
- **Romance** - Love stories, relationship drama
- **Mystery** - Whodunits, detective stories
- **Thriller** - Suspense, action, edge-of-your-seat plots
- **Horror** - Scary stories, psychological thrillers
- **Adventure** - Journeys, exploration, treasure hunts

### Advanced Reading Interface

**Scene-by-Scene Navigation**
- Sidebar with full story structure
- Jump to any scene instantly
- Independent scroll areas (navigate without losing your place)
- Reading progress tracking (pick up where you left off)

**Immersive Reading Mode**
- Auto-hide UI on scroll (distraction-free)
- Tap or scroll up to show controls
- Sticky bottom navigation for quick access
- Dark mode support for late-night reading

**Cross-Device Sync**
Your reading history syncs across all devices. Start on mobile, finish on desktop.

### Discovery Features
- **Trending Stories**: See what's hot right now
- **Featured Content**: Curated picks from the community
- **Story Statistics**: Views, ratings, word counts
- **Genre Filtering**: Find exactly what you're in the mood for

### Engagement
- **Comment System**: Leave thoughts on stories, chapters, or scenes
- **Rating System**: Rate individual scenes and whole stories
- **Like/Favorite**: Show appreciation for great content
