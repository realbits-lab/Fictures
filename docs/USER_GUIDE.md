### Using AI Assistance

**What AI Can Help With:**
- **Generate Story Outlines** - Get plot structure and chapter breakdowns
- **Create Characters** - Generate character profiles with backstories
- **Write Scenes** - AI-powered scene content generation
- **Improve Writing** - Get suggestions for better dialogue and descriptions
- **Generate Images** - Create character portraits and scene illustrations

**How to Use AI:**
1. Click the AI button (ğŸ¤–) in the editor
2. Choose your task (outline, character, scene, improve)
3. Provide context or prompts
4. Review and edit AI-generated content
5. Iterate until you're happy with the result

**Pro Tips:**
- AI works best with clear, specific prompts
- Always edit AI content to match your voice
- Use AI as a collaborator, not a replacement
- The more context you provide, the better the output

### Character Management

**Creating Characters:**
1. Go to "Characters" tab in your story
2. Click "Add Character"
3. Fill in details (name, role, personality)
4. Generate AI portrait (optional)

**Character Grid:**
- Visual overview of all your characters
- Quick access to character details
- Track character appearances across scenes
- AI-generated portraits

### Publishing Your Work

**Manual Publishing:**
1. Navigate to a chapter or scene
2. Click "Publish" button
3. Your content becomes visible to readers

**Automated Publishing:**
1. Go to "Publish" (ğŸ“¤) in the menu
2. Click "Create Schedule"
3. Choose schedule type:
   - **Daily** - Release every day at set time
   - **Weekly** - Specific day of the week
   - **Custom** - Every N days
   - **One-Time** - Schedule a specific date
4. Select scenes/chapters to publish
5. Set it and forget it!

### Analytics

**Track Your Performance:**
1. Go to "Analysis" (ğŸ“Š) in the menu
2. View metrics:
   - **Total views** - How many people read your story
   - **Engagement** - Likes, comments, ratings
   - **Reading time** - How long readers spend
   - **Drop-off points** - Where readers stop
3. Get AI-generated insights and recommendations

### Joining Discussions

**Where to Find Discussions:**
1. Click "Community" (ğŸ’¬) in the menu
2. Browse recent posts across all stories
3. Or visit a specific story's community page

**Post Types:**

1. Navigate to a story's community page

- Be respectful and constructive
- Spoiler-tag reveals

- Share copyrighted content

**Moderation:**

- Inappropriate content will be flagged/removed

## Tips & Tricks

### For Writers

**Writing Productivity:**
- Set a daily word count goal
- Write scenes in order for better flow
- Use AI to overcome writer's block
- Save frequently (auto-save is on, but still)

**Story Quality:**
- Keep scenes focused (one main event per scene)
- Show, don't tell (use dialogue and action)
- Create compelling characters with clear goals
- End chapters on hooks to keep readers engaged

**Growing Your Audience:**
- Publish consistently (use automated scheduling)
- Engage in community discussions
- Cross-promote in story descriptions
- Share on social media

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"I can't see my published content"**
- Check if you clicked "Publish" button
- Verify you're not in draft mode
- Clear browser cache and reload

**"AI generation is slow"**
- AI processing can take time (especially for long content)
- Be patient - quality generation takes a few minutes
- Check your internet connection

**"My images aren't loading"**
- Images might still be generating
- Check browser console for errors
- Try hard refresh (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + R)

**"Reading progress isn't syncing"**
- Make sure you're signed in
- Check internet connection
- Try logging out and back in
